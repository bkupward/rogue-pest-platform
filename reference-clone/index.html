Have Questions?

We've got the answers

What makes Rogue Pest Solutions different from other pest control services in Alabama?

We're locally owned and family operated, with customized treatments, eco-friendly options, and a satisfaction guarantee on every visit.

How often should I have my home or business inspected for pests?

Most homes do best on a quarterly program, with one-time or monthly options available.

Are Rogue Pest Solutions and control treatments safe for my pets and family?

Yes — we use EPA-approved, family- and pet-friendly products applied responsibly.

What are the signs that I might have a termite problem?

Mud tubes, discarded wings, hollow-sounding wood, and bubbling paint.

How can I prevent mosquitoes from breeding on my property?

Empty standing water weekly, clean gutters, and keep grass trimmed.

What should I do if I find rodent activity in my home?

Avoid disturbing droppings and call us — we trap, remove, seal, and sanitize.

Can Rogue Pest Solutions help with spider infestations?

Yes — we treat harborage areas, remove webs and egg sacs, and reduce their food sources.

What environmentally friendly pest control methods does Rogue Pest use?

Targeted baiting, exclusion, and integrated pest management that minimizes product use.

How quickly can I expect results after a pest control treatment?

Many pests are knocked down within the first visit; established infestations may need a follow-up.

What are the most common pests in Greater Birmingham and throughout Alabama?

Termites, mosquitoes, fire ants, cockroaches, spiders, and rodents.
